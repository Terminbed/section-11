## 3. Recovery Framework

### Auto-Deload Rules

- HRV ↓ >20% → reduce intensity  
- RHR ↑ ≥5 bpm → remove intensity  
- Feel ≥4 → endurance only  
- ≥2 triggers → full recovery  

---

## 4. Performance Targets

### Short-Term (8–12 weeks)

- FTP: +5–8%  
- Long ride decoupling: <5%  
- Reduce Z3 (grey zone) accumulation  

---

### Medium-Term (6–12 months)

- Improve durability at current FTP  
- Increase sustainable volume at same recovery cost  
- Target ~4.2 W/kg

## 6. Key Constraints

- Strength training adds systemic fatigue  
- High volume → recovery is limiting factor  
- Mixed terrain → pacing variability  

---

## 7. Adaptation Focus

- Threshold repeatability  
- Durability (fatigue resistance)  
- Maintain aerobic volume  
- Avoid excessive Z3
