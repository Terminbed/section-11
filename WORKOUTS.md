# Workout Generation Rules

## General

* Use power targets (based on FTP = 275W)
* Include warm-up and cooldown
* Include clear interval structure
* Keep total duration realistic for the day

## Naming

Format:
[TYPE] - [FOCUS] - [DURATION]

Example:
Threshold - 2x20 - 1h30

## Structure Example

Warm-up:
- 10 min @ 55–70% FTP

Main:
- 2 x 20 min @ 95–100% FTP
- 5 min recovery between

Cool-down:
- 10 min easy

## Rules

* No grey-zone filler
* Match readiness (modify = reduce intensity)
* Respect weekly structure (long rides weekend)
* Do not stack intensity days
